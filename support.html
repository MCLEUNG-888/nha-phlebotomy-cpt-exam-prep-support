Welcome to Support

Thank you for using Phlebotomy CPT Exam Prep.

This page provides help for the App's study tools, question experience, progress features, Premium access, subscriptions, and purchases.

1. Study Progress and Saved Work

The App is designed to save supported study progress locally on your device so you can return to your learning activities.

Depending on the study mode and feature you are using, saved information may include:

  • Study progress
  • Completed questions
  • Practice results and scores
  • In-progress study sessions
  • Saved or bookmarked questions
  • Incorrect-answer review history
  • Domain performance
  • Study guide progress

2. Returning to a Previous Question

Supported question flows may allow you to navigate back to a previous question during an active study or practice session.

Use the available navigation controls within the question interface to move between supported questions without intentionally ending the session.

3. Reviewing Incorrect Answers

The App includes study tools designed to help you revisit questions you answered incorrectly.

Use the Wrong Answer Review or related review area available within the App to revisit missed questions and reinforce weaker topics.

4. Saved and Bookmarked Questions

You may save or bookmark supported questions for later review.

Saved-question features are intended to help you create a focused review list while preparing for your examination.

5. Study Guides and Domain Progress

The App may provide structured study guides covering phlebotomy-related domains and learning topics.

Study guide completion is tracked separately from practice question accuracy where supported.

This allows you to distinguish between content you have reviewed and your performance on related practice questions.

6. Premium Access

The App may offer Premium access that unlocks additional study content, questions, practice tools, mock exams, or other Premium features.

Available Premium purchase options may include:

  • Monthly Premium: US$13.99 per month
  • Three-Month Premium: US$29.99 every three months
  • Lifetime Premium: US$49.99 one-time purchase

The price displayed by Apple in the App Store purchase interface is the authoritative price for your country or region.

7. Restore Purchases

If you previously purchased Premium but your Premium access is not showing correctly, first use the Restore Purchases option inside the App.

Make sure you are signed in to the appropriate Apple Account associated with the original eligible purchase.

Purchase restoration is processed through Apple's App Store services.

8. Subscription Management

Monthly Premium and Three-Month Premium are intended to be auto-renewable subscriptions.

Subscription billing, renewal, cancellation, and payment management are handled through Apple.

You can manage eligible subscriptions through the subscription settings associated with your Apple Account.

Deleting the App does not automatically cancel an active subscription.

9. Lifetime Premium

Lifetime Premium is intended as a one-time non-consumable purchase and does not automatically renew.

If an eligible Lifetime Premium purchase is not recognized, use Restore Purchases while signed in to the Apple Account associated with the original purchase.

10. Purchase or Billing Problems

Apple processes App Store purchases, subscription payments, billing, and eligible refund requests.

For problems involving a payment method, duplicate charge, billing transaction, subscription charge, or refund request, you may need to use Apple's official purchase support services.

11. Premium Access Is Not Appearing

Try the following:

  1. Confirm that your device has an internet connection.
  2. Confirm that you are signed in to the correct Apple Account.
  3. Open the App and select Restore Purchases.
  4. Close and reopen the App.
  5. Confirm that your subscription is active in your Apple Account settings.
  6. Install the latest available version of the App.

12. App Is Not Working as Expected

If you experience an unexpected issue, try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the App.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Check that your device is running a supported operating system version.
  4. Check for an App update in the App Store.
  5. Confirm that sufficient device storage is available.

Be cautious before deleting or reinstalling the App because locally stored study progress may be lost if it is not preserved by an available backup or restoration mechanism.

13. Language Support

The App may provide study content or interface support in multiple languages.

English is the primary source language for educational content. Localized content may also be provided in supported languages.

If you notice incorrect, mixed, missing, or mismatched translations, please report the affected screen, question, study guide section, and selected language.

14. Reporting a Question or Content Issue

We welcome reports about possible educational content issues.

When reporting a question, please provide as much of the following information as possible:

  • Question identifier, if visible
  • Domain or study section
  • The selected language
  • A description of the possible issue
  • A screenshot, when appropriate

Please do not send confidential examination questions or unauthorized examination content.

15. Reporting a Technical Problem

For technical support, useful information may include:

  • Your device model
  • Your operating system version
  • The App version
  • The feature or screen affected
  • Steps that reproduce the problem
  • A screenshot, when appropriate

Do not include passwords, complete payment card information, private medical records, or other unnecessary sensitive information in a support request.
