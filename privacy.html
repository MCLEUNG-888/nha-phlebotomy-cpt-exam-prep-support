Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Phlebotomy CPT Exam Prep application.

Effective Date: July 19, 2026
Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how the Phlebotomy CPT Exam Prep application ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App.

The App is designed as an independent educational study and examination-preparation resource. We aim to minimize data collection and keep your study activity on your device whenever reasonably possible.

1. Information We Collect

The current version of the App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information in order to access its core study features.

We do not operate an account database containing your name, home address, phone number, or payment card information.

2. Study and App Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store information locally on your device to provide its study and personalization features. Depending on the features you use, locally stored information may include:

  • Study progress and completion status
  • Practice question results and scores
  • Incorrect-answer or review history
  • Saved or bookmarked questions
  • In-progress study or quiz sessions
  • Domain or topic performance information
  • Study guide progress
  • App preferences and language settings

This information is used to provide features such as saved progress, performance review, wrong-answer review, bookmarks, and personalized study feedback.

Under the current App configuration, this study information is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to or stored in a developer-operated user account or cloud database.

3. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App may offer Premium access through Apple In-App Purchase, including subscription options and a one-time lifetime purchase.

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and Apple's StoreKit services. We do not directly collect or store your complete payment card number or banking information.

The App may receive purchase-related information from Apple that is necessary to determine whether Premium access is active, restore eligible purchases, and provide purchased functionality.

Apple's handling of information is governed by Apple's own privacy policies and terms.

4. Restore Purchases

The App provides a Restore Purchases function for eligible purchases associated with your Apple ID.

Restoration and purchase verification are handled through Apple's App Store services. We do not receive your Apple ID password or complete payment information.

5. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The current version of the App is not designed to include third-party advertising or cross-app advertising tracking.

We do not sell your personal information.

The current version of the App does not use third-party advertising identifiers for behavioral advertising.

If analytics, crash-reporting services, advertising services, cloud synchronization, or other services that materially change our data practices are introduced in a future version, this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated as appropriate.

6. Device Permissions

The App does not require access to sensitive device features such as your precise location, contacts, microphone, camera, or health records for its core exam-preparation functionality.

If a future feature requires additional device permissions, the App will request permission through the operating system and explain the purpose of the request where required.

7. Support Communications

If you voluntarily contact us for support, you may provide information such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or a description of the issue you are experiencing.

Information voluntarily submitted for support purposes will be used to respond to your request, troubleshoot the reported issue, improve support, and maintain appropriate support records.

Please do not include sensitive medical, financial, or other unnecessary personal information in a support request.

8. How We Use Information

Information handled by the App may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Save your study progress on your device
  • Display scores and performance information
  • Support review and bookmark features
  • Determine Premium access status
  • Restore eligible App Store purchases
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the App
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

9. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Information may be processed by Apple when you download the App, make an In-App Purchase, manage a subscription, or restore a purchase. Those interactions are subject to Apple's applicable privacy practices and terms.

We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, legal process, or lawful government requests, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the App, or others.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Study progress and related information stored locally on your device generally remain on the device until you reset or remove the relevant data through available App features, clear the App's data where supported, or uninstall the App.

Removing the App may permanently remove locally stored study information unless your device or operating system separately preserves or restores that information through a device backup.

Information voluntarily provided through a support request may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain support records, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations.

11. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures intended to protect information under our control.

However, no electronic storage system, device, or method of transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You are responsible for maintaining appropriate security for your device, Apple ID, passwords, and device backups.

12. Children's Privacy

The App is intended as a professional certification exam-preparation and educational resource and is not directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children under 13 through the App.

If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 has been provided to us in a manner that requires deletion, we will take reasonable steps to delete it as required by applicable law.

13. Educational and Medical Disclaimer

The App provides educational and exam-preparation content only.

The App does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, emergency guidance, or professional healthcare services. Information in the App should not be used as a substitute for professional medical judgment, institutional policies, official training, or applicable laws and regulations.

14. Independent Application

Phlebotomy CPT Exam Prep is an independent educational exam-preparation application.

The App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially connected with the National Healthcareer Association (NHA).

References to certification examinations, credentials, or certification organizations are used solely to describe the educational purpose of the App.

15. International Users

If you use the App from outside the United States, applicable privacy and data-protection rights may vary depending on your jurisdiction.

We will handle information under our control in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App, our practices, legal requirements, or platform requirements.

When material changes are made, the revised policy will be published on this page with an updated effective or last-updated date.

Your continued use of the App after an updated policy becomes effective is subject to the revised policy and applicable law.
